Design Notes

  • MVP is always yesterday's winner - uses Bayesian Average scoring to reward sustained performance over lucky streaks

  • MVP stays fixed - doesn't change when toggling days
  • Bayesian formula: (g/(g+10)) * ratio + (10/(g+10)) * 0.5
